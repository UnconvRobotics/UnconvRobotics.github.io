Overview

What it is about?

The 2nd Unconventional Robotics Symposium is a free two-day event at TU Delft for researchers, students, and technically-minded enthusiasts who take robotics beyond its standard assumptions; through soft bodies, physical computation, embodied intelligence, nonlinear dynamics, and inspiration from biology, arts, and beyond.

Free for all. No registration fee.

Why this event?

Academic robotics conferences are valuable - but they are also expensive, carbon-heavy, and often inaccessible. This symposium was born as a different model: a focused, free, local event where researchers and enthusiasts can exchange ideas without the overhead. No registration fee. No need to fly. Just good talks and good discussion.
